FIXING "INVALID ORIGIN" ERROR - STEP BY STEP
---------------------------------------------

STEP 1: GO TO YOUR DNS PROVIDER
   - Open your browser
   - Go to where you bought fntx.ai (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, etc.)
   - Log in to your account

STEP 2: FIND DNS SETTINGS
   - Click on your domain fntx.ai
   - Look for "DNS", "DNS Management", or "Manage DNS"
   - Click it

STEP 3: ADD A RECORD
   - Click "Add Record" or "Add" button
   - Choose "A" from the dropdown
   - Fill in:
     * Type: A
     * Name: @ (this means fntx.ai)
     * Value: 35.194.231.94
     * TTL: 3600 (or leave default)
   - Click "Save" or "Add Record"

STEP 4: WAIT 5-30 MINUTES
   - DNS needs time to update

STEP 5: GO TO GOOGLE CLOUD CONSOLE
   - Open https://console.cloud.google.com/
   - Click your project

STEP 6: FIND YOUR OAUTH CLIENT
   - Click "APIs & Services" in left menu
   - Click "Credentials"
   - Click on your OAuth 2.0 Client ID

STEP 7: ADD AUTHORIZED ORIGINS
   - Under "Authorized JavaScript origins"
   - Click "ADD URI"
   - Type: http://fntx.ai:8080
   - Click "ADD URI" again
   - Type: http://localhost:8080
   - Click "SAVE" at bottom

STEP 8: USE YOUR DOMAIN
   - Close the IP address tab (http://35.194.231.94:8080)
   - Open new tab
   - Go to: http://fntx.ai:8080
   - Google Sign-in will now work
